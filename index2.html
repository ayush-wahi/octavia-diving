Start Your Diving Adventure

Discover Scuba Diving

Never dived before? This is your first breath underwater. Learn basic safety skills, experience real ocean diving under professional supervision, and decide if scuba is for you — no prior experience required. Perfect for beginners who want a safe and unforgettable introduction.

PADI Open Water Course

Become a certified scuba diver recognised worldwide. Learn essential diving skills, underwater navigation, equipment use, and safety procedures. Once certified, you can dive up to 18 metres anywhere in the world. This is your passport to global adventure.

Take Your Diving to the Next Level

PADI Advanced Open Water Course

Build confidence and expand your skills. Improve navigation, buoyancy, and complete deep and adventure dives. Dive deeper (up to 30m) and experience more challenging underwater environments. Perfect for divers ready to grow.

Deep Specialty

Explore the deep safely and confidently. Learn gas management, narcosis awareness, and emergency procedures. Unlock access to deeper dive sites and experience dramatic underwater landscapes.

Wreck Specialty

Discover sunken ships and hidden underwater history. Learn wreck penetration techniques, line handling, and safety planning. Perfect for adventurous divers who want to explore beyond the reef.
